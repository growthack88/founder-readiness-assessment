## [1.0.0] — 2024

### 🎉 Initial Release

#### Added
- 8 assessment dimensions covering all key founder readiness areas
- 64 questions in Arabic with English dimension labels
- 4-tier founder archetype system (Explorer → Builder → Launcher → Ready Founder)
- Animated radial chart for overall score display
- Animated dimension bars with score breakdown
- Strengths & areas-for-improvement panels
- Personalized advice tied to book content
- RTL Arabic layout with bidirectional support
- Bilingual UI (Arabic primary, English secondary labels)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Dark theme with dimension-specific color coding
- Zero-dependency, single-file architecture
- No backend, no data collection, no cookies

#### Dimensions Included
1. 🧠 Founder Mindset (عقلية المؤسس)
2. 💡 Idea Clarity (وضوح الفكرة)
3. 👥 Team & Culture (الفريق والثقافة)
4. 🚀 Product & Market (المنتج والسوق)
5. ⚡ Leadership & Decisions (القيادة واتخاذ القرار)
6. 💰 Business Model & Funding (نموذج العمل والتمويل)
7. ⚖️ Balance & Sustainability (التوازن والاستدامة)
8. 🤖 AI Readiness (الجاهزية للذكاء الاصطناعي)
